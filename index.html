INSTRUCTIONS

  • Place tiles on the board to spell out words. Click on the letter on your rack and then click again on the location you'd like to place it.
  • Except for the first turn, at least one of your letters must touch a letter already in play, and your new letters must all be in the same row or column, and be touching another tile.
  • The letter values of each tile can be accessed by clicking the tab on the right side of the screen.
  • At the beginning of your turn, click "SHOW TILES" to reveal the letters on your rack.
  • After you click "Submit Word" your score is tallied and your rack refreshed from the tile bag.
  • Clicking "Back to Rack" will return the tiles from your current turn back to your rack.
  • Clicking "Refresh Tiles" will give you a brand new rack and shuffle your current tiles back into the bag. Doing this forfeits your current turn.
  • When one player clears their rack and the bag is empty, the game ends. The other player's score will decrease by the total value of the letters still in his/her rack.
  • Enter both player's names and click "START" to begin!
